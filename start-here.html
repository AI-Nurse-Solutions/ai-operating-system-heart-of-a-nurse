Take the 2-Minute SOUL Quiz

Why first: your values and boundaries — not your tech skills — decide how AI should support you. The quiz asks about what matters to you, what stresses you, and what AI must never touch. It drafts your personal SOUL file. Nothing leaves your browser.

Map your Life & Projects (10 minutes)

Your review of systems. Where does your real life need help — studying, time, documentation habits, family logistics, wellness, a community project, a business idea? This map makes sure AI helps where it actually matters, and includes a life-coverage check so nothing important gets missed.
