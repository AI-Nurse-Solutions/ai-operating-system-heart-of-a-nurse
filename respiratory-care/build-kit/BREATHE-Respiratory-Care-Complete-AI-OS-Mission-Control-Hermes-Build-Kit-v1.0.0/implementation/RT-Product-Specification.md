## Purpose and exclusions

The product supports RT-owned whole-life planning, professional learning, source verification, fictional simulation, credential reminders, communication rehearsal, career and negotiation preparation, leadership, quality/scholarship planning, ethical enterprise/community exploration and recovery. It does not practice, prescribe, diagnose, credential, bill, represent, approve or act for the Respiratory Care Professional.

It is not a patient record, EHR, clinical decision-support, triage, diagnosis, treatment, prescribing, ordering, documentation, coding, billing, claims, prior-authorization, credentialing, payer, legal, employment, research-determination, surveillance or institutional execution system.

## Mission loop and artifact truth

Every mission supports Assess, Diagnose/Define, Plan, Implement and Evaluate with save, history, pause, branch, reopen, measures and new cycles. Implement means prepare local tasks, questions, drafts and fictional prototypes. Real action occurs outside the product through accountable humans and approved official systems.

The exact artifact lifecycle is `Exploration → Simulation → Recommendation → Draft Artifact → Approved Plan → Authorized Execution → Completed Action → Evaluated Outcome`. Approved Plan is human scope review. Authorized Execution is an external reference gate. Completed Action requires verified external evidence.

## Offline and AI behavior

Missions, projects, tasks, notes, measures, sources, artifacts, Guide, capability evidence, search, archive, export/import, backup, restore and deletion work without AI. AI controls stay disabled or Setup required until a real backend passes authenticated health and capability checks. Connected status requires recent evidence, not configuration or page load.

## Completion

A nondeveloper can install, personalize, complete and reopen a synthetic mission, restart without data loss, inspect permissions, use Pause All, back up, restore and uninstall. Operational additionally requires genuine configured streaming and cancellation through the packaged UI plus all applicable execution evidence.
