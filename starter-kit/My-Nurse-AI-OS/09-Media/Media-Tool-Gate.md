# Media Tool Gate

Use before enabling image or video generation.

## Questions

1. What is the image or video for?
2. Is the output personal, professional, public, or commercial?
3. Does the prompt contain PHI, patient identifiers, screenshots, confidential employer information, or private family details?
4. Could the image imply clinical authority, diagnosis, endorsement, or safety claims?
5. Which model/backend is being used?
6. What is the expected cost?
7. Where will the output be stored?
8. Will it be published publicly?
9. Who reviews it before use?
10. How will source prompts and final assets be documented?

Default boundary:

> Media tools are for learning, design drafts, dashboards, and public-safe educational artifacts — not patient care, clinical documentation, or PHI-bearing material.
