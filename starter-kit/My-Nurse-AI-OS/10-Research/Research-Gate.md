# Research Gate

Use before connecting new research tools, MCP servers, scheduled scans, or external APIs.

## Questions

1. What research lane is this: personal knowledge, academic/medical, or technical project?
2. What source systems are being used?
3. Is there PHI, patient-specific context, confidential employer material, or private personal information?
4. What claims need original-source verification?
5. What is the output format and where will it be saved?
6. Will this become a reusable skill?
7. Is a scheduled cron job appropriate, or should this remain manual?
8. What tool permissions or MCP tools are actually needed?
9. What tools should be excluded?
10. Who reviews the final synthesis before use?

Default boundary:

> Research tools gather, compare, organize, and synthesize. Humans verify, judge, apply, and steward.
