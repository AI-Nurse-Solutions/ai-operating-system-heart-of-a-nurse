# Nurse AI Side-Gig Starter Kit

**Position:** the first doorway into NIN/NAIO for nurses who want to explore AI-enabled side projects safely, without handing over judgment or touching patient information.

> Build your first safe, no-PHI nurse-AI project in 30 days — with prompts, templates, guardrails, and a guided operating system.

## Who this is for

- Nurses and nursing students curious about AI but unsure where to begin.
- Bedside nurses exploring a side project, content lane, workshop idea, tutoring offer, workflow template, or community resource.
- Nurse educators, leaders, and builders who want a low-risk starting point before cohorts, pilots, or advanced NAIO tracks.

## What you get

1. `EDENA-No-PHI-Guardrails.md` — the safety boundary before you build.
2. `Side-Gig-Offer-Canvas.md` — one-page offer design for nurse-centered AI ideas.
3. `30-Day-Validation-Sprint.md` — daily plan from idea to first conversations.
4. `Prompt-Pack.md` — no-PHI prompts for ideation, packaging, content, and review.
5. `Content-Calendar.md` — 30 days of trust-building posts.
6. `Landing-Page-Copy.md` — simple launch-page copy for a first offer.
7. `Outreach-Conversation-Script.md` — human-first validation script.

## The doorway

This kit is not the whole NAIO journey. It is the gentle first doorway:

**Starter Kit → Lamp Huddle → NIN community → Founding Steward Cohort → Builder Academy / EDENA governance pathway.**

## Boundary

No PHI. No patient-specific clinical decisions. No employer secrets. No certification claim. Human judgment first.

Use this kit for learning, reflection, public-safe content, workflow templates, community building, and no-PHI entrepreneurship. Do not use it for patient-care advice, diagnosis, treatment, medication, triage, charting, staffing decisions, institutional compliance claims, or clinical deployment.

Carry the lamp. Keep the ledger. Agents propose. Humans judge. Nurses steward.
