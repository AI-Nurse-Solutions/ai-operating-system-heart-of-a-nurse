# Outreach Conversation Script

Use this to validate the idea with humans before building too much.

## Ask for the conversation

Hi [Name] — I am testing a safe, no-PHI Nurse AI Side-Gig Starter Kit for nurses who want to explore AI projects without crossing patient-care or privacy boundaries. Could I ask you 5 quick questions? No patient stories or employer secrets needed.

## Five questions

1. Where do nurses like you feel curious about AI but unsafe or overwhelmed?
2. What kind of side project would feel useful: template, prompt pack, workshop, content system, study tool, or community resource?
3. What would make you trust or distrust a nurse-AI product?
4. What boundary language would you need to see before using it?
5. If this kit existed, would you download it, pay for it, join a workshop, or ignore it? Why?

## Close

Thank you. I am keeping this no-PHI and non-clinical. I am using the feedback to decide whether to build a small first version, not to make clinical or certification claims.
