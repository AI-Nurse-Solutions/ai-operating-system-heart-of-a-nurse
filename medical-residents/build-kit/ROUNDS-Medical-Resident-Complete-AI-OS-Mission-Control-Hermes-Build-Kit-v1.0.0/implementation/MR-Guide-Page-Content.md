
# Guide — Using `My ROUNDS`

## What this is

`My ROUNDS` is a private resident-controlled planning, learning, rehearsal, and whole-life workspace. It helps organize questions, sources, fictional simulations, drafts, and human checkpoints. It does not practice medicine, know your patient, determine supervision, evaluate you, or act for your program or institution.

## Before entering anything

Use public sources, unmistakably fictional cases, minimum nonsensitive resident-owned plans, and abstract non-reconstructable reflection. Never paste a real/name-removed case, chart, screenshot, image, recording, sign-out, consult, note, order, event, M&M, formal evaluation, test-bank item, credential secret, or restricted institutional material.

## Personalization

Discover and Soul adapters contribute only approved derived fields. Review field-level provenance and uncertainty before saving. Raw quiz answers and raw `SOUL.md` remain outside the app. Program, PGY, role, attending, and supervision fields are context—not authority.

## Navigate the one home

Use the Core Four: Orient My Day & Duty, Learn & Reason, Orchestrate & Communicate, Review, Escalate & Close. Five protected record scopes organize the work inside this home. A mission has one primary hat and may have one secondary hat. Select one scope and one private partition; least privilege wins.

## Start a mission

1. **Assess:** screen data; run ATTEND; identify sources, assumptions, duty/recovery state, EDENA, and unknowns.
2. **Define:** state the learning/planning need, non-goals, human decision owner, safe output, and stop condition. This is not clinical diagnosis.
3. **Plan:** compare options, public/synthetic sources, CIRCLE/ORBIT needs, burden, accessibility, fallback, expiry, and review.
4. **Implement preparation:** create local tasks, questions, drafts, fictional rehearsals, or one approved P1/P2 run. Do not carry out care or institutional action.
5. **Evaluate:** record human review, source integrity, uncertainty, usefulness, burden, accessibility, failure, and Retain–Revise–Pause–Remove.

You can pause, branch, return to an earlier stage, or reopen a mission. Material changes invalidate downstream approval.

## ATTEND, CIRCLE, and agents

ATTEND keeps activity/hat, training/entrustment, team/attending, evidence/data, escalation, and decision/destination visible. Missing, stale, expired or conflicting authority is **Unverified — authorized human confirmation required** and produces only questions for the accountable human route, never a patient-care answer.

CIRCLE is fictional/process-only here. All 24 powers install inactive. Preview one at a time. Ten agents install P0. Personal one-run ceiling is P2. P3/P4 require a different separately provisioned institutional system and cannot be enabled here. P5 is prohibited.

Private whole-life material (`MR-DATA-W`) is manual/local only: do not send health, family, relationship, finance or other whole-life details to a model or agent. The Day & Capacity and Career & Whole-Life agent names do not override this rule.

## Capabilities and recovery

Capability evidence reflects safe AI stewardship only. It never proves clinical competence or permission. Use Pause All when state, content, authority, or destination is uncertain. Safe Reset returns agents, tools, connectors, memory additions, schedules, and actions to Off. ROUNDS removal preserves the foundation; Full Uninstall preserves only the approved export and unrelated work.
