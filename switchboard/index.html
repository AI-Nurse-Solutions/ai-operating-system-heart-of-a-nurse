A living role library

The launch roles are a beginning—not a ceiling

The registry separates professional or community roles, temporary assignments, reusable capabilities, and future context adapters. DISCOVER and FUTURE are proposed universal taxonomy labels in this preview; they are distinct from the existing standalone public distributions. New public roles require classification, source and claim review, EDENA review, synthetic tests, and independent review. Local roles stay visibly unreviewed.

Draft role

Medico-Legal / Legal Nurse Consultant

Public-source review, synthetic chronology templates, evidence organization, and question framing. No legal advice, expert-witness status, case authority, or records access is inferred.

Draft role

Nurse Informaticist

Workflow maps, requirements drafts, terminology review, and governance preparation. No production EHR, configuration, procurement, or deployment authority is inferred.

No-PHI agent maps, review queues, stop rules, and escalation design. No agent execution, hidden delegation, connector activation, or autonomous clinical action.

Community listening, coalition stewardship, participatory development, resource navigation, public education, and safe tool building. No mandate, automatic outreach, fundraising, or political authority is inferred.
