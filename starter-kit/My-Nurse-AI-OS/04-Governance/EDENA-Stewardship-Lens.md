# EDENA Stewardship Lens

Use for major recommendations.

## Equity and Ethics
Who benefits? Who could be harmed or excluded?

## Dignity and Data
What data is involved? Is dignity protected?

## Environment and Externalities
What downstream effects could occur?

## Nursing Relevance and Nurse Wellbeing
Does this support nursing judgment, workflow, and wellbeing?

## Agency and Action
Who is accountable? What action is safe, reversible, and human-led?
