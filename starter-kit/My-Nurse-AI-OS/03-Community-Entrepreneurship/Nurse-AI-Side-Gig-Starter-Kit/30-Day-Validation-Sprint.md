# 30-Day Nurse AI Side-Gig Validation Sprint

Goal: validate one safe, no-PHI nurse-AI offer before building a large product library.

## Week 1 — Listen before building

- Day 1: Pick one audience: student, staff nurse, educator, leader, or nurse entrepreneur.
- Day 2: Write the pain in their words.
- Day 3: Draft one safe promise using the Offer Canvas.
- Day 4: Run the EDENA guardrail check.
- Day 5: Ask 3 nurses what they would want help with — no patient stories.
- Day 6: Write 5 public-safe content ideas.
- Day 7: Decide: continue, narrow, or stop.

## Week 2 — Build the minimum useful kit

- Day 8: Create the folder outline.
- Day 9: Draft the one-page guide.
- Day 10: Draft 10 prompts.
- Day 11: Add safety boundaries to every page.
- Day 12: Create a simple landing-page promise.
- Day 13: Ask 3 nurses to review the promise.
- Day 14: Revise based on their words.

## Week 3 — Test in public, gently

- Day 15: Post the problem, not the product.
- Day 16: Share one useful checklist.
- Day 17: Invite replies from nurses who want the first version.
- Day 18: Hold 2 validation conversations.
- Day 19: Create a simple PDF or template.
- Day 20: Ask for one paid or waitlist commitment.
- Day 21: Decide whether the signal is strong enough.

## Week 4 — Package the doorway

- Day 22: Name the offer.
- Day 23: Finalize the no-PHI version.
- Day 24: Add the “what this is not” boundary.
- Day 25: Create a 5-email or 5-post launch sequence.
- Day 26: Invite 10 people manually.
- Day 27: Deliver the first version.
- Day 28: Ask what was confusing.
- Day 29: Document what worked.
- Day 30: Choose the next path: Lamp Huddle, NIN community, Founding Steward Cohort, Builder Academy, or pause.

## Success criteria

Validated means at least one of these:

- 10 nurses say the problem is real.
- 5 nurses request the kit.
- 1 nurse pays, pre-orders, or commits to a workshop.
- A leader or educator asks how to bring it to a group.

Do not claim clinical outcomes, ROI, certification, or readiness. Track adoption signals and learning evidence only.
