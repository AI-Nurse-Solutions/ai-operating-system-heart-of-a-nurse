The Life & Projects Quiz

Your SOUL file taught your AI who you are. This quiz maps what you're carrying — your whole life across 3 spheres and 17 domains — and turns each active area into a governed project prompt your system can actually work on. About ten minutes.

This quiz lives entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded, no account, no tracking — at the end you download your project files; we never see them. Close the tab and it's gone. And as everywhere here: no PHI, no clinical decisions, ever.

Haven't taken the SOUL Quiz yet? Do that first — projects work best on top of your values and boundaries.
