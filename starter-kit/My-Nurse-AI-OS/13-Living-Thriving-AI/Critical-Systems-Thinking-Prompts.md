# Critical and Systems Thinking Prompts

Use AI by questioning it.

## Safe-answer prompt

```text
Before answering, list what information would be needed to make this safe and complete.
Then separate what is known, assumed, uncertain, and what needs human judgment.
```

## Systems lens prompt

```text
Analyze this AI suggestion through a systems lens.
What could go wrong for workflow, staffing, equity, patient experience, or team communication if we trusted it blindly?
```

## Student teaching strategy

```text
Think first. Consult AI second. Compare. Correct. Reflect.
```

## AI critique round

```text
Review this AI-generated answer as a nurse educator.
Identify correct claims, unsafe claims, missing context, bias, assumptions, and what evidence or guideline should be checked.
```

## 3 AM nurse test

```text
Would this AI output help or burden a bedside nurse at 3 AM?
Assess clarity, safety, workflow fit, cognitive load, and escalation needs.
```
