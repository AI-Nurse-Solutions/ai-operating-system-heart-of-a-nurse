The decision

One runtime. One governed control plane.

Nurse AI OS is a no-PHI, nurse-governed control plane over the Hermes Agent runtime. Hermes supplies the horizontal runtime; EDENA supplies deterministic risk and autonomy semantics; Florence-X supplies bounded orchestration; human approval remains the authority boundary; and tamper-evident evidence makes the system's claims inspectable.

Hermes remains the only agent runtime. OpenClaw, LangGraph, OpenAI Agents SDK, Google ADK, Microsoft Agent Framework, PydanticAI, Letta, and OpenHands were studied for patterns—not installed as parallel orchestration layers. This avoids duplicating profiles, context, skills, memory, tools, approvals, cron, delegation, Kanban, and gateways already present in Hermes.

Boundary: Harness 2.0 does not authorize PHI, patient-specific clinical decisions, EHR access, named-personnel decisions, payments, credential handling, or unreviewed external action. It is not a HIPAA audit, security certification, clinical product, or institutional approval.

Read it by role

What each executive should inspect

CNO · human authority

  • AI advises and drafts; nurses and authorized humans judge.
  • Red means stop/escalate—not autonomy.
  • Patient, chart, personnel, and policy-adoption decisions stay human or institutional.
  • Nurse Steward Council review remains a release gate.
  • No patient-outcome or compliance claim is made.

CIO · effective control

  • Capabilities compile across profile, audience, channel, provider, sandbox, trust cell, and runtime.
  • Every layer may narrow; none may re-grant.
  • Unknown fields and unmanifested tools fail closed.
  • Metadata-only, hash-chained evidence supports audit without retaining prompts or payloads.
  • Profiles are not host sandboxes; enterprise isolation remains required.

CTO · technical assurance

  • Typed, content-hashed capability manifests.
  • Hermes-native pre-tool and result-transform hooks.
  • Hash-bound proposals instead of suspended model state.
  • Restart-safe side-effect handling, budgets, model-routing limits, and connector manifests.
  • Versioned synthetic trajectory datasets and machine-readable release evidence.

Canonical semantics

Risk and autonomy are different questions

DimensionValuesMeaning
Risk tierGreen · Yellow · Orange · RedConsequence and escalation. Red means prohibit, stop, or route to an authorized human/institutional process.
Autonomy levelA0 · A1 · A2 · A3 · A4How independently a bounded capability may operate. A4 is reserved and unavailable in self-service Nurse AI OS.

Invariants: risk never grants authority; autonomy never lowers risk; ambiguity moves risk upward and autonomy downward; a child agent or downstream configuration may narrow but never restore a denied capability.

Honest status

Known · Assumed · Unknown · Recommended · Decide

Known

The source executes successfully in the tested canary; Hermes loaded the plugin; 44 unit tests and eight synthetic trajectories passed; the signed Phase 23 bundle and default profile were not changed.

Assumed

The host operator remains trusted; Hermes native approval behavior remains consistent with the tested version; any institution will add identity, endpoint, network, retention, procurement, labor, legal, privacy, and incident-response controls.

Unknown

Institutional concurrency, broad adversarial false-positive/false-negative rates, nurse usability burden, council acceptability, and organization-specific regulatory requirements remain to be established.

Recommended

Use public or synthetic data in a dedicated profile and isolated workspace; expand adversarial evaluations; review redlines with nursing, privacy, security, legal, informatics, and affected workers; reevaluate after any model, tool, manifest, policy, or connector change.

Decide

The matching Phase 23 private key was restored at its original protected path and its derived public fingerprint matched the trusted RSA key. Harness 2.0's exact release-evidence bytes are signed with the existing anchor; the anchor was not rotated. The detached signature is independently verifiable. This cryptographic integrity proof does not create clinical, regulatory, council, or institutional approval.
