Executive decision

Keep the default profile in shadow mode

Nurse AI OS now has a signed Hermes-native governance runtime and a live six-lane English post-setup distribution. It does not yet have a coherent end-to-end enforcement architecture.

Do not activate canary enforcement until EDENA semantics are unified, the six-lane handoff is durably represented beyond local Setup Helper state, shadow evidence is adjudicated, pre-tool and post-generation pathways are threat-modeled, rollback is exercised, and the exact canary scope receives human authorization.

Priority architecture gaps

Four P0 issues must be closed before enforcement

PriorityGapRequired response
P0The repository EDENA policy conflicts with the signed Harness semantics; Red cannot mean both stop and high autonomy.Adopt one two-axis risk/autonomy schema and enforce it in CI.
P0SOUL normalizes four roles while the English post-setup layer has six lanes. Setup Helper captures the user's lane locally, but the exported handoff artifact does not persist it.Add a separate post_setup_lane handoff field without treating it as identity or credential verification.
P0PHI or live-care content can enter before a tool call or through memory, media, channels, delegation, or model output.Produce an end-to-end no-PHI threat model and adversarial path tests.
P0Enforce-capable configuration could be promoted without valid governance authorization.Require a dedicated profile, startup attestation, exact-artifact/scope approval, promotion record, and tested rollback.

The full report also documents incomplete tool mapping, the absence of a demonstrated general post-generation delivery gate, local-ledger tamper limits, authority-laundering risk, multi-user isolation requirements, Council status, and the separate private-corpus consent discrepancy.

Non-claims

What this report does not establish

  • Clinical safety, patient outcomes, or clinical readiness
  • HIPAA compliance, a BAA, or permission to process PHI
  • Institutional, procurement, legal, regulatory, or deployment approval
  • Nursing licensure, employment, competency, or authority from role selection
  • Complete mediation of every input, memory, tool, channel, or output path
  • Nurse Steward Council approval or external security certification
Bedside test: The system can help prepare the work. It is not authorized to take a nurse’s judgment, accept patient data, or act beyond the approved scope. Today, the governor is watching and learning; it is not yet blocking.
