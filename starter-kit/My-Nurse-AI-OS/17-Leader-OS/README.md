# Leader OS — the Governance Sprint, made agentic

The leader pathway's promise is a nurse manager with a **defensible method** — not a vendor demo. This pack is that method as a working system: skills that draft your unit communications for your approval, scaffold QI work, analyze only what's safe to analyze, keep a decision ledger, and make you the unit's AI-governance leader with the review-gate machinery from the Governance Kit at your fingertips (`governance-kit/` — grab it from the Resource Hub's Safety & governance section if you don't have it yet).

**Setup (2 minutes).** Tell your AI: *"Load the six skills in 17-Leader-OS, including the Leader-Boundary-Header they share. Apply my Do-Not-Remember rules with the Manager/Leader extension. Then open First-Governed-Workflow-Week.md and start Day 1."*

**The rules that make this safe to lead with.**
- **People decisions are never the AI's** (🔴, permanently): hiring, evaluation, discipline, and anything that affects an individual's employment is a human decision, humanly delivered.
- **Aggregates only.** Skills here work on anonymized, aggregate, non-identifiable data. Named-staff performance analysis is refused by design — proxy metrics launder bias into people decisions.
- **Staff-facing and org-facing communication is 🟡 minimum** — drafted, then approved by you before anyone else sees it.
- **Employer-data integrations are 🟠 deferred**: no HRIS, EHR, scheduling-system, or employer Teams/Slack connections without institutional/compliance approval, routed through the [vendor & integration questions](../08-Integrations/Skill-and-MCP-Vetting-Checklist.md).
- If you've approved every AI draft unchanged for a month, that's not efficiency — that's a rubber stamp. `Governance-Facilitator` will say so (the Governance Kit's gate-health rule).

**Outcomes worth tracking** (adoption signals only — never staffing-outcome, safety-outcome, or compliance claims): governed workflows in use, gate health (edits and rejections vs. unchanged approvals), time-to-draft you reclaim, decision-journal completeness.

> Agents propose. Humans judge. Nurses steward. · No PHI, no personnel files, ever.
