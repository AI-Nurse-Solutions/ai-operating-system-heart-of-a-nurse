# Nurse Educator Case Study Flow

## Green path — non-PHI / fictional

1. Create a fictional case in an approved course planning document.
2. Ask AI to draft learning objectives, debrief questions, quiz items, and rubric language.
3. Review for accuracy, bias, safety, and scope.
4. Store the final teaching version in the approved course folder.
5. Label it as fictional or composite.

## Yellow / Red path — real clinical material

Stop before using external AI if the source includes:

- patient name, initials, MRN, DOB
- precise dates or room numbers
- rare condition plus unique story details
- photos, voice, family identifiers
- identifiable staff/student data
- employer confidential information
- operational details not approved for external sharing

Escalate to organizational privacy, security, legal, or governance review.

## Prompt for fictional case creation

```text
Create a fictional nursing education case for [topic].
Do not use real patient details.
Include learning objectives, brief scenario, student tasks, debrief questions, safety concerns, and evaluation rubric.
Make the case realistic but not identifiable.
```
