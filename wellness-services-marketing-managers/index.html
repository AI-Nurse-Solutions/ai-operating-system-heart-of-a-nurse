THRIVE operating framework

Six disciplines for responsible wellness growth

Each pillar supports reviewable preparation. None grants clinical, legal, claims, marketing-platform, financial, employment, procurement, or institutional authority.

T — Truth & Trust

Separate facts, assumptions, unknowns, approved claims, sources, disclosures, and qualified review.

H — Human-Centered Experience

Design accessible service journeys without exploiting fear, illness, vulnerability, identity, or sensitive traits.

R — Reach & Relationships

Prepare channel and partnership options without person-level targeting, hidden profiling, outreach, or contact automation.

I — Integrated Campaigns & Operations

Coordinate drafts, owners, dependencies, gates, expiry, and reconciliation without becoming the official system.

V — Value & Viability

Explore transparent scenarios and burden without guaranteeing revenue, outcomes, demand, savings, or return.

E — Ethics, Evidence & Agents

Keep sources inspectable, decisions human-owned, and all ten optional agents disabled until separately tested and approved.

Permanent safety ceiling

No clinical advice, manufactured evidence, sensitive targeting, or consequential action

Do not submit PHI; patient, client, lead, contact, attendee, referral, employee, contractor, or testimonial-subject records; raw health stories; credentials; payment-card data; proprietary lists; confidential contracts; secrets; or merely name-stripped narratives.

THRIVE does not diagnose, recommend care, promise health or business outcomes, fabricate citations or testimonials, infer protected or sensitive traits, profile vulnerability, or decide who should receive a health-related message. It does not contact people, publish, advertise, send, schedule, boost, bid, change price, spend, transact, sign, hire, evaluate, discipline, procure, change security, or write an official system.

Institutional use is a separate state. It requires authorization for the exact organization, role, purpose, platform/model, data, claims, systems, connectors, actions, logging, retention/deletion, security, human owners, incident response, and fallback. Download or private-workspace installation grants none of this.

Consent before mutation

Inspect first. See the whole card. Decide afterward.

  1. Use a new or verified-isolated workspace. Nursing, resident, respiratory, research, and hospital-administration state must not be shared.
  2. Give Hermes the entire one-file program. Issue only INSPECT THRIVE INSTALLER ONLY — CREATE NO STATE.

  4. Approve, revise, install foundation only, or cancel. Silence, download, access, credentials, earlier work, or generic interest is never approval.
  5. After approval only: foundation → 40 S1 checks → inactive THRIVE overlay → 120 S2 checks → evidence-bearing Activation Report.
