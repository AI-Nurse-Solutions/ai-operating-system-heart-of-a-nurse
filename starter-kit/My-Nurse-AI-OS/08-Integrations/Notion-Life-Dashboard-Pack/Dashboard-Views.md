# Suggested Notion Views

Use these views after importing the CSV databases.

## Health & Wellbeing

- **Today** — Date is today
- **Energy Trends** — Board by Energy Level
- **Sleep Review** — Table sorted by Sleep Hours
- **Weekly Reflection** — Calendar view

## Finances

- **This Month** — Month is current month
- **Bills & Due Dates** — Type is Bill, sorted by Due Date
- **Savings / Debt Progress** — Board by Status
- **Needs Human Decision** — Human Gate is Yes

## Goals

- **Active Quarter** — Status is Active
- **By Sphere** — Board by Sphere
- **Needs Next Action** — Next Action is empty or Review Date is on/before today
- **Completed Wins** — Status is Complete

## Habits

- **Daily Habits** — Cadence is Daily
- **Weekly Habits** — Cadence is Weekly
- **By Energy Cost** — Board by Energy Cost
- **Needs Simplifying** — Status is Paused or Human Gate is Yes

## Tasks

- **Today** — Status is Today or Due Date is today
- **This Week** — Due Date is within next week
- **Waiting / Blocked** — Status is Waiting or Blocked
- **Human Gate** — Human Gate is Yes

## Routines

- **Morning / Evening** — Time Block is Morning or Evening
- **Weekly Reset** — Frequency is Weekly
- **Low-Energy Version** — Minimum Viable Version is not empty
- **Needs Review** — Review Date is on/before today
