# Builder OS — the Side-Gig Starter Kit, made agentic

The [Nurse AI Side-Gig Starter Kit](../03-Community-Entrepreneurship/Nurse-AI-Side-Gig-Starter-Kit/README.md) is the content spine — offer canvas, 30-day validation sprint, content calendar, outreach script, no-PHI guardrails. This pack turns that spine into a working system: skills that run the sprint on a schedule, draft with your calendar, research with guardrails, and check every offer against the boundaries before it ships.

**What's inside**

**Setup (2 minutes).** Tell your AI: *"Load the six skills in 16-Builder-OS, including the Builder-Boundary-Header they share. My side-gig project file is [Projects/<your-slug>.md from the Life & Projects Quiz, or describe it]. Start the Validation Sprint at Week 1 and schedule the weekly check-in."*

**The rules that make this safe to sell from.**
- Everything customer-facing is 🟡 — drafted, then approved by you before it leaves.
- **Payments, contracts, and pricing decisions are yours alone** (🔴 for the AI, always). Automated payment or outreach is 🟠 deferred — the same boundary as the governed installer's commercial pack.
- No clinical-advice products without a licensure/scope review; no certification claims; no outcome guarantees. `Boundary-Counsel` enforces the wording.
- **Read the FTC primer before your first client.** HIPAA probably doesn't cover your side gig — which means *you* are the privacy program, and the FTC still has jurisdiction. Client details stay session-only (see DO-NOT-REMEMBER, entrepreneur extension).

> Agents propose. Humans judge. Nurses steward. · No PHI, no secrets, ever.
