Step 3

What to do after downloading

  1. Install Hermes Desktop. Use the macOS or Windows installer for your computer. Keep the installer in a place you can find again.
  2. Take the SOUL Quiz. The quiz drafts your personal AI operating posture and exports your no-PHI setup files. Take the SOUL Quiz →
  3. Open the starter kit. Use the folder architecture, safety checklist, dashboards, and first prompts as your starting system.
  4. Begin Day 1 of the roadmap. Do not try to build the whole system in one sitting. One safe workflow is enough for the first day. Open the roadmap →
  5. Choose your dashboard. Use Notion if it fits your life, or use the browser-only local HTML dashboard when you need a no-account fallback.
  6. Only then consider the full governed build. Read the NAIO OS guide before using advanced installer or harness steps. Read the governed OS guide →

Quick verification idea

On macOS, technical users can compare a download hash with:

shasum -a 256 Hermes-Setup.dmg

On Windows PowerShell:

Get-FileHash .\Hermes-Setup.exe -Algorithm SHA256

The safety perimeter

No PHI. No clinical decisions. No silent automation.

Do not enter patient names, charts, screenshots, identifiers, room numbers, employer secrets, or patient-specific clinical details into Hermes, the quizzes, dashboards, or any AI tool unless an approved institutional environment and accountable governance explicitly allow it.

Hermes can help with learning, organization, drafting, research support, project planning, and reflection. It does not carry a license, validate competency, approve clinical deployment, replace educators, or make patient-care decisions.
