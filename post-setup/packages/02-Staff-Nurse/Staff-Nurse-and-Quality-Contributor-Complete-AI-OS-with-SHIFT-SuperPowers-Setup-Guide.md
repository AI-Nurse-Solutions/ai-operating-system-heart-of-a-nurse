## What SHIFT is

SHIFT is a nurse-controlled life, practice, communication, learning, career, and quality-improvement support environment. It keeps the original Staff Nurse Life & Practice foundation, adds governed frontline-quality tools, and embeds everything in the existing Command Center.

It does not replace clinical judgment, the charge nurse, provider, pharmacist, rapid-response or emergency process, official event reporting, EHR, quality department, data steward, policy owner, HRPP or IRB, privacy or security team, union or labor route, HR, legal counsel, or organizational approval.

**Promise:** Own your practice. Improve the system. Protect the life beyond the shift.

## SHIFT means

- **S — Safeguard** practice, privacy, personal readiness, and recovery.
- **H — Humanize** communication, teamwork, advocacy, and peer learning.
- **I — Improve** quality through just, evidence-informed frontline action.
- **F — Flourish** in learning, career, wellbeing, relationships, and life.
- **T — Trust** governed AI, authorized data, transparent measures, and reversible automation.

## Choose the right role adapter

- **Direct-Care Staff Nurse:** personal planning, general learning, deidentified reflection, communication rehearsal, and quality-idea preparation; no authority to launch change.
- **Unit Champion, Preceptor, or Shared-Governance Member:** adds only documented contributor duties; it does not create management, discipline, staffing, competency, or peer-scoring authority.
- **Chartered Staff-Nurse QI Project Lead:** prepares work only inside a sponsor-approved charter and exact decision rights.
- **Hybrid or Multiple-Employer:** shows the active hat and employer on each consequential artifact. Switching contexts reruns the authority and data checks.

## Two separate deployment contexts

1. **Private Personal Mode — default:** no institutional data, PHI, patient or event narrative, coworker detail, restricted patient-safety material, employer connection, or real measure export. Personal readiness, health, recovery, relationships, finances, and life goals stay private.
2. **Institution-Approved Quality Workspace — separately authorized:** only when the organization explicitly provisions and governs it. It needs a sponsor, staff-nurse role, QI or research human determination, data-steward receipt, approved aggregate or properly deidentified data, small-cell rules, role access, release owner, retention, deletion, and auditability. Minimum-necessary sponsor or decision-owner work contact metadata may be used for authorized routing; coworker performance, health, private, inferred, and narrative information remain prohibited. Installing SHIFT does not create or certify this workspace, and binding it transfers zero private-life records.

- **Overlay removal:** removes SHIFT powers and overlay records while preserving the Staff Nurse foundation and unrelated work.
- **Full uninstall:** previews restoration of S0 and preserves unrelated work.
- **Sensitive-data event:** stop, do not repeat the content, prevent further use or retention where controls allow, and use the authorized clinical, privacy, security, event-report, quality, research, HR or labor, or legal response route.
