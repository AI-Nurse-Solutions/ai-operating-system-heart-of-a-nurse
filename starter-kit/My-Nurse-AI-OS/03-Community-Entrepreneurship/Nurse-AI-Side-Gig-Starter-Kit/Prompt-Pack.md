# Nurse AI Side-Gig Prompt Pack

Use these prompts only with no-PHI, public-safe, non-clinical material.

## 1. Find the safe problem

```text
Act as a nurse-centered entrepreneurship coach. Help me identify safe, no-PHI side-gig ideas for nurses.

My audience is: [student nurses / staff nurses / nurse educators / nurse leaders / nurse entrepreneurs]
Their burden is: [describe without PHI or employer secrets]

Give me 10 ideas that are education, productivity, workflow, reflection, or community support only. Do not include patient-specific clinical advice, diagnosis, treatment, medication, triage, staffing decisions, certification claims, or clinical deployment claims.
```

## 2. Sharpen the buyer

```text
Help me narrow this nurse-AI side-gig idea to one buyer and one urgent problem.

Idea: [idea]
Audience: [audience]
Boundary: No PHI, no clinical decisions, no certification claim.

Return:
1. The specific buyer
2. The painful moment
3. The safe transformation
4. What this offer must not promise
5. 5 questions to ask in validation conversations
```

## 3. Build the offer promise

```text
Draft 10 one-sentence promises for this no-PHI nurse-AI offer.

Offer: [describe]
Buyer: [describe]
Boundary: no PHI, no patient-specific clinical decisions, no employer secrets, no certification claim, human judgment first.

Make the language warm, plain, and nurse-centered. Avoid hype and income guarantees.
```

## 4. EDENA risk review

```text
Review this side-gig idea through EDENA: Equity & Ethics, Dignity & Data, Environment & Externalities, Nursing relevance & Nurse wellbeing, Agency & Action.

Idea: [paste]
Audience: [paste]
Claims I might make: [paste]

Flag risks, overclaims, missing boundaries, and safer wording. Classify Green / Yellow / Orange / Red. Suggest a safer next version.
```

## 5. Landing-page draft

```text
Create a simple landing page for this no-PHI nurse-AI offer.

Include:
- Headline
- Subheadline
- Who it is for
- What they get
- What it is not
- Boundary statement
- CTA to email or join waitlist

Tone: secular-but-reverent, practical, warm, nurse-centered. No tech-bro language. No clinical claims.
```

## 6. Content calendar

```text
Create a 30-day content calendar for this nurse-AI side-gig idea.

Goal: build trust and invite 10 validation conversations.
Audience: [audience]
Offer: [offer]
Boundary: no PHI, no clinical decisions, no certification claim.

For each day, give: topic, hook, 3 bullets, CTA, and safety note if needed.
```

## 7. Human review before publish

```text
Review this asset before I publish it.

Asset: [paste]

Check for:
- PHI risk
- clinical advice risk
- employer-secret risk
- certification or readiness overclaim
- hype or income guarantee
- unclear human judgment boundary
- confusing CTA

Return only: publish / revise / stop, with reasons and safer language.
```

Carry the lamp. Keep the ledger.
