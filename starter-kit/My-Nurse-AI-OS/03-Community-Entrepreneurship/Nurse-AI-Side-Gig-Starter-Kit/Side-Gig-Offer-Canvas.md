## 3. Safe promise

Write a one-sentence promise that does **not** imply clinical decisions, certification, or guaranteed outcomes.

> This helps [specific nurse] do [safe no-PHI task] so they can [human benefit], while keeping judgment and patient-care decisions with humans.

## 4. Product format

Choose one:

- PDF workbook
- Notion / Obsidian template
- Prompt pack
- Mini-course
- Workshop
- Checklist pack
- Local HTML dashboard
- Community challenge

## 5. Proof you can gather safely

- 10 nurse conversations
- Waitlist signups
- Replies to one LinkedIn post
- Workshop attendance
- Pre-orders
- Qualitative feedback
- Before/after self-reported confidence — not clinical outcomes

## 6. Boundary

No PHI. No patient-specific clinical decisions. No employer secrets. No certification claim. Human judgment first.

## 7. Next smallest step

The next step is not “build the business.” It is:
