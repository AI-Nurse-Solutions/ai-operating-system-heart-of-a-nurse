
# Guide — Using Your Respiratory Care Professional BREATHE Mission Control

## What this is

`My BREATHE` is a private planning, learning and professional-development workspace. It helps you organize missions, investigate questions, prepare drafts, run fictional simulations, supervise bounded AI and evaluate what changed. It does not provide patient care or professional authority.

## Personalization

The Discover and Soul adapters contribute only approved derived fields. You see and edit the proposed Mission Profile before saving it. Raw quiz answers and raw `SOUL.md` are not stored. Update or delete your profile as your life and work change.

## Workspaces, hats and contexts

The five workspaces organize life/practice, evidence/learning, credentials/career, leadership/impact and whole-life/venture work. Select one task hat and one personal, employer, academic, business or community context for each mission. These selections help routing; they do not prove authority.

## Start a mission

Use **Start a Mission** to describe a nonsensitive goal, situation, project or decision. Choose a workspace, context and success measure. The loop remains visible:

1. Assess — facts, user input, sources, assumptions, constraints and unknowns.
2. Diagnose/Define — the central need or opportunity; not a clinical diagnosis.
3. Plan — compare options, burden, ethics, authority, measures and safeguards.
4. Implement — create local tasks, rehearsals, private drafts and fictional prototypes.
5. Evaluate — compare results, record limitations and decide Continue, Revise, Pause, Stop, Escalate or start another cycle.

You may pause, branch, return to an earlier stage or reopen a completed mission.

## BREATHE and agents

Twenty-four powers are installed inactive. Preview at most one in the first week. Activation requires an exact card, synthetic/low-sensitivity preview, SCOPE and explicit approval. Supervised success permits review, not automatic promotion.

Agents install disabled. A P1/P2 agent runs once under a visible charter and returns to disabled. Stop ends the selected run; Kill terminates it and records recovery. P3 is available only as institution-approved read or sandbox access inside an exact authorized nonclinical scope; it has no write authority. The target ships with no executor. P4 is unavailable in the Personal Edition and for every clinical, device or patient-contact destination. Only a separately governed institutional extension may stage one exact nonclinical P4 action for named-human review and release. P5 is prohibited.

## EDENA

Green is low-risk private support. Yellow requires review. Orange is consequential preparation only. Red presents a prominent warning. A non-absolute Personal Red may lead only to a new clean fictional exploration after deliberate acknowledgment. Institutional Red remains blocked. An absolute stop cannot be waived.

## Do not enter

Do not enter patient information, real or possibly real clinical narratives, chart/order/result/message/claim content, clinical images/audio, diagnosis/treatment/triage/prescribing/dosing requests, credential secrets, controlled-substance identifiers, passwords/tokens, restricted employee/learner/research records, full private contracts or confidential institutional data.

## Evidence and badges

Check source identity, version, applicability, conflicts and uncertainty. Review artifacts before use. Badges require eligible evidence and are never professional credentials or permission to practice.

## Recovery

Use Pause All when uncertain. Keep backups and checkpoint receipts. Safe Reset returns tools, agents, connectors, memory additions, schedules and external actions to off. BREATHE removal preserves the foundation. Full uninstall preserves only your approved export and unrelated work.
