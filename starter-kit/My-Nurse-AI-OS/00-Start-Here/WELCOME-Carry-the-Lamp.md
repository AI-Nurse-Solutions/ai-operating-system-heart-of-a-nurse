# Start Here: Carry the Lamp into the AI Age

> *"She was the lady with the lamp. She was also the first to chart the truth in a way the powerful could not ignore — and the truth saved lives."*

Florence Nightingale carried two instruments through the Crimean wards: a lamp and a notebook. She stayed present with the suffering and protected human dignity at the bedside — and she gathered data, built her famous rose diagram, and proved that most deaths were preventable. Compassion told her to act; evidence told everyone else they had to.

This starter kit hands you both instruments for today: **data science and AI, wielded with the heart of a caring, stewarding nurse.** Build a calm, governed operating system that carries the busywork so you can carry the patient.

## Who this is for

### The student nurse
- **The ache:** drowning in readings, care plans, and NCLEX prep; tempted to let AI think for you.
- **The lamp:** a Learning OS, "think first then consult AI" habits, and a calm system for deadlines and wellbeing.
- **Who you become:** a nurse who learns deeply and reasons independently — AI as tutor, never crutch.

### The staff nurse
- **The ache:** buried in charting, hand-offs, and overload; less time at the bedside than on the screen.
- **The lamp:** a Chief-of-Staff OS, safe no-PHI drafting/summarizing workflows, and ergonomics and focus habits.
- **Who you become:** a nurse who reclaims time and presence — the system carries the busywork.

### The nurse leader / educator
- **The ache:** asked to "do AI" without a method; worried about safety, privacy, equity, and adoption.
- **The lamp:** a governance method (HERMES, EDENA, profiles, gates), Team Lab and workshop blueprints, and tool-evaluation questions.
- **Who you become:** a Nightingale for your unit — evidence-led, dignity-first AI without losing the heart of nursing.

## How to use this kit

1. Read `MY-AI-VALUES.md`, `NO-PHI-BOUNDARY.md`, and `HUMAN-AGENCY-RULES.md` first.
2. Set up Hermes safely with `Hermes-Setup-Safety-Checklist.md`.
3. Post-setup suggested step: if Notion is unavailable, open the **Local HTML Life Dashboard** at `08-Integrations/Local-HTML-Life-Dashboard/index.html` for health, finances, goals, habits, tasks, and routines; keep it No PHI, create a browser bookmarks folder named **Dashboards**, and bookmark the local dashboard there.
4. Build your spheres (Personal, Professional, Community) using folders 06–07.
5. Add integrations, media, research, messaging, and visual workflows (folders 08–12) only after the no-PHI foundation is solid.
6. Protect yourself and your team with folders 13–14 (wellbeing, Workspace + HIPAA).
