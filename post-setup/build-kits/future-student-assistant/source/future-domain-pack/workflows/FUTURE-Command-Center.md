# FUTURE Command Center

Maintain one Command Center with Focused, Standard, and Expanded views. The opening view shows no more than seven attention items.

## Core Four launchers

1. **Plan My Next Move** — today, week, capacity, minimum mode, and one human conversation.
2. **Learn & Practice** — retrieval, teach-back, fictional scenarios, skills-lab preparation, and role-appropriate review.
3. **Check It with SAFE AI** — classify data and authority, verify sources, inspect bias, and create an integrity receipt.
4. **Build My Future** — portfolio, career, bridge education, network, community, and 90-day plan.

An optional fifth launcher remains empty until separately previewed and approved. The FUTURE Library is secondary and all powers begin inactive.

## Required views

- Role, active context, North Star, current season, and capacity mode
- Today's Top Three and minimum/re-entry plan
- Learning targets, retrieval schedule, misconceptions, and human questions
- Clinical or skills readiness using only approved generic or fictional content
- AI Literacy Passport with evidence and next practice
- Career, portfolio, opportunity, mentorship, and community horizon
- Sources, freshness, uncertainty, and disclosure status
- Approvals and human-review queue
- FUTURE Library and activation state

## Permanent controls

Privacy & Permissions; Active Context; Academic/Employer Rules; Review Sources; AI Use & Disclosure; Activity History; Pause All; Safe Reset; Correct; Export; Delete; Rollback; Uninstall.

Every card shows purpose, context, approved inputs, prohibited inputs, source status, risk tier, human authority, current mode, next step, external-action status, memory status, and stop conditions. Use **Unknown**, **Not configured**, **Stale**, or **Awaiting verification** instead of blanks. No dark patterns, shame, rankings, or decorative AI theater.
