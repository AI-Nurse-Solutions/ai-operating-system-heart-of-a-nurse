Source ceilings are maximum proposals, never installed permissions. Choose no agent when local controls suffice. A visible router suggests at most one primary agent and explains why; the resident may choose no-agent mode. A second frozen reviewer gets no hidden context and cannot approve its own work.

`MR-DATA-W` whole-life content—including private health, family, relationship and finance material—remains manual/local only and is never sent to a model or agent. AGT-01 and AGT-10 may use only their enumerated `MR-DATA-0/1/2` subsets; `MR-DATA-M/R` control metadata also remains outside every model and agent payload. Agent names do not widen data admission, partitions, memory, badges or exports.

Every one-run ORBIT charter binds owner, purpose, profile, hats, scope, partition, data, sources, model, tools, time/token/cost/retry/concurrency limits, output, EDENA, human gates, stop/kill/fallback, expiry, retention, and receipt. One invocation, zero hidden retries, no recursion, and automatic return to P0 are required.

P3 and P4 are schema-visible but technically unavailable in the personal target. P5 and every clinical, EHR, order, event, evaluation, message, page, handoff, consult, patient, or institutional execution path are prohibited.
