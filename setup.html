Two doors into your Nurse AI OS

There are two safe ways in, and both lead to the same system. Almost everyone starts with Door 1 — no installation, working tonight in the browser AI you already use. Door 2, the full governed Hermes build, is optional and can wait as long as you like.

Door 1 · Most nurses start here

Start today — no installation

Use the browser AI you already have (ChatGPT or Claude). Take the SOUL Quiz, download the Starter Kit and workbook, and do steps 2–5 of Start Here by pasting instead of uploading. You get the same personalized system — SOUL file, folder home, dashboard, first prompts — with nothing installed and nothing to maintain.

Choose Door 1 when: you want a win tonight, your computer can't run Hermes yet, or you'd rather prove the workflows before committing to new software.

Door 2 · Optional, later is fine

The full governed build — install Hermes

Hermes is the free, open-source AI engine (by Nous Research) that makes your system permanent: local files, standing instructions, memory that sticks. Install it only from the official site, with the Pre-Procedure Checklist beside you, then continue the same five steps. The governed Nurse AI OS layer — dry-run first, self-test, undo/recovery, and human approval on every gate — comes after your basics feel steady.

Choose Door 2 when: your Door-1 workflows are proven and you want them permanent, private, and on your own machine.

Switching doors is normal — in both directions. Door 1 nurses add Hermes when a real need shows up. Door 2 nurses still use browser AI on the go. Your SOUL file, Starter Kit, and dashboard travel with you either way, so nothing you build tonight is ever wasted.

The rules that protect everyone

Plain-language safety rules

You memorized the rights of medication administration. These are shorter, and they protect your patients, your license, and you — behind either door, in any AI tool.

No patient data. Ever.

No names, charts, screenshots, room numbers, record numbers, dates, or stories that could identify a patient — in any AI tool, at any level. If you're unsure whether something counts: it counts. Leave it out.

AI drafts. You decide.

Every AI output is a draft until a human reviews it. Nothing gets sent, published, or acted on without your judgment.

Your license is not outsourceable.

No AI here supports clinical decisions, assessments, or patient-specific care questions. It carries your busywork — never your judgment.

Employer systems stay at work.

No EHR screens, no confidential policies, no identifiable staff or student data. Public, fictional, or personal material only — unless your organization's compliance team has explicitly approved a tool and workflow.

When in doubt, ask a human.

Bring gray areas to the Lamp Huddle or email great.ai.nurses@gmail.com. A question asked early beats an incident reported late.

These rules have a name: EDENA — Ethical Design & Enablement for Nursing Augmentation — the governance framework in every layer of Nurse AI OS. Every action gets classified before it runs: 🟢 Green — may proceed 🟡 Yellow — you approve first 🔴 Red — human authorization mandatory You'll meet it formally in the workbook — for now, the five rules above are all you need. When you're ready to make your AI follow them automatically, download the Governance Kit — the rules as files your system loads every session.
