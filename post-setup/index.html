What happens after a self-install build-kit download?

  1. Keep the original ZIP unchanged in a private local folder.
  2. Start Hermes in a new or verified-isolated workspace and give it the complete ZIP.
  3. Hermes performs read-only package, environment, authority, data-boundary, compatibility, and rollback checks. It installs nothing during this preflight.
  4. Hermes presents one exact Implementation Activation Card. You choose Approve, Revise, or Cancel.
  5. If you cancel or withhold approval, Hermes makes no installation mutation. If you approve, Hermes performs only the written scope in visible phases and gives you a receipt after each phase.
  6. The final handoff states Operational, Core operational; AI setup pending, or Not operational from actual target-environment evidence—not from a prompt, mockup, or written test list.

Never place PHI, patient-linked narratives, identifiable workforce records, personnel files, restricted organizational material, credentials, or secrets in the package or implementation conversation.

A role choice is personalization—not verification. These downloads do not prove nursing licensure, nursing-assistant certification, NP certification, population focus, prescriptive authority, privileges, enrollment, employment, instructional-design assignment, teaching or grading authority, scope, delegation, supervision, competence, faculty status, managerial authority, accreditation authority, institutional approval, or permission to access patient, student, employee, or personnel information.
