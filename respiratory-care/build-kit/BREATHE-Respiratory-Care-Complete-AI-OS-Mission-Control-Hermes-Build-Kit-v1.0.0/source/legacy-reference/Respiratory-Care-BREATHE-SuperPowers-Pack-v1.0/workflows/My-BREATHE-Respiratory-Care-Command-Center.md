# My BREATHE — Respiratory Care Command Center

Create exactly one responsive, accessible home in `respiratory_care`. Do not place it inside, under or beside another population page and do not share state.

## Core Four

1. **Orient Shift & Capacity** — role, site, assignment status, SCOPE, Top Three, capacity, workload questions, recovery and Plan B.
2. **Learn & Verify** — sources, generic evidence, synthetic cases, device learning, credentials and source freshness.
3. **Coordinate Care & Equipment** — synthetic or approved CIRCLE preparation, equipment readiness, rounds, consults, transitions, teaching and quality.
4. **Review, Escalate & Close** — SCOPE receipts, human decisions, acknowledgments, official destination, agents, expiry, fallback and controls.

Keep the optional fifth launcher empty until separately previewed and approved. Opening view: seven attention items maximum.
